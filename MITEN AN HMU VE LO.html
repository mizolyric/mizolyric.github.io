LALTANPUIA TOCHHAWNG

MITEN AN HMU VELO



Bawihte i hmangaihna aw nem chuan in,

Ka thinlung hliam a thawi dam thin;

I ngilneihna leh rinawmna te chuan,

Nitin min ti hlim thin.



I anka nem chuan min ti lawm thin a, I len thla leh kimtlang ah chuan;

Hmangaihna eng zun a lo zam chiai e,

Miten an hmu ve lo.



Hmangaihna rinawm lo hian di karah,

Thinlung a ti hliam si thin a;

Mahse, nang zawng i rinawmna vang hian,

Thinlai a phang ve lo.



Damlai hringnun kawng chhuk chho zawh tur leh,

Lungngaih buaina hmachhawn turin;

Tunah hraileng te chawi ve tawh turin,

I tiam ve tawh em le?



Ka hmangaihna hian ka ngen ta che a

I thinlai pawh tui ang dam se;

Sial ang ka seilenna run chhungah hian,

Kumtluang in par vul rawh.
